About Us

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Somal-ot is a youth-led organization dedicated to fostering leadership, community development, and active participation among the youth. As the official governing youth council, we serve as a bridge between the younger generation and local government, ensuring that the voices of the youth are heard and valued in decision-making processes. At SK Barangay Somal-ot, we believe that the youth play a vital role in shaping a progressive and sustainable community. Our initiatives focus on leadership development, civic engagement, sports, education, environmental awareness, and cultural preservation. Through collaboration with various stakeholders, we continue to create opportunities for young individuals to grow, contribute, and make a lasting impact.

OUR MISSION

Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Somal-ot is committed to the mission of offering the younger generation a tranquil society that is devoid of the risk associated with sexuality transmitted infections, substance abuse, domestic agression, child mistreatment, gambling, and other negative influences adjustments misconducts. This objective is pursued through engaging community-centered intiatives primarily centered on education, sports, youth advancement, and safeguarding the sorroundings.

OUR VISION

The young residents of Barangay Somal-ot will be acknowledged by the local community for their capabilities and will be given various chances for involvement in civic activities, service, leadership, education acquisition, training, life skills, gender-sensitive and development, as well as peace and equality promotion, thus serving as an inspiration for the upcoming generation.









Organizational Structure

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Somal-ot is composed of dedicated youth leaders who work together to create meaningful programs and initiatives for the community. Our structured leadership ensures efficient planning, decision-making, and execution of projects that promote youth development, civic engagement, and social responsibility.
